Hi, I'm Virgar.

Frequently asked

Do you come to my home?

Yes — most jobs are done on-site in and around Tórshavn. For simple problems I can also help remotely over the phone or a secure screen-share.

What if you can't fix the problem?

Then you don't pay for my time. If a part needs replacing or a specialist is needed, I'll tell you honestly and help you find the right person.

Do you sell computers?

No. I don't resell hardware, which means my advice stays honest. I'll happily help you choose the right laptop or phone from a local shop.

Is my data safe?

Always. I never copy personal files off your device, and anything I see stays between us. Backups are always made before risky repairs.

Can you help an elderly parent who lives alone?

Yes, and this is some of my favourite work. I set things up so they feel confident, and I'm only a phone call away if something goes wrong later.
